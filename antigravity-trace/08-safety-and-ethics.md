# Safety and Ethics

- all data is synthetic/anonymized
- no real patient information is used
- no real MR numbers, phone numbers, or hospital identifiers are included
- no real appointments are booked
- no real caregiver messages are sent
- no diagnosis or treatment is provided
- the system is a hackathon prototype only
- real-world use would require clinician oversight, authentication, privacy controls, audit logs, and regulatory compliance
