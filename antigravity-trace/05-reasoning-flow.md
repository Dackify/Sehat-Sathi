# Reasoning Flow

**Input:**
Synthetic high-risk renal deterioration case.

**Extracted facts:**
- diabetes
- hypertension
- worsening creatinine
- low eGFR
- high potassium
- anemia
- repeated admissions
- missed nephrology follow-up
- medication safety concern

**Reasoning:**
- renal function has worsened significantly
- potassium is high-risk
- repeated admissions suggest unstable disease control
- no nephrology review for 8 months is a continuity-of-care failure
- metformin with very low eGFR requires clinician review
- care coordination should be escalated

**Decision:**
Risk classified as HIGH.

**Action:**
Generate urgent nephrology review, medication safety review, repeat labs, caregiver alert, renal diet education, and follow-up reminder.

**Execution:**
Simulate ticket creation, appointment scheduling, alert generation, dashboard update, and reminder creation.

**Outcome:**
Patient state changes from passive record to active care coordination.
